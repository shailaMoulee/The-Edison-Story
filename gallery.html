#WeAreTheLight

Thomas Edison CTE High School offers a diverse range of programs and pathways designed to prepare students for success in the modern workforce. Our cutting-edge STEM initiatives provide students with opportunities to explore emerging technologies, conduct hands-on experiments, and engage in innovative projects that challenge their problem-solving skills.

In addition to our STEM offerings, we offer comprehensive career and technical education pathways in fields such as engineering, health sciences, culinary arts, and more. These programs provide students with practical skills and industry certifications that enhance their college and career readiness.

With a focus on academic excellence, our school has achieved notable accomplishments, including high scores on standardized tests, prestigious scholarships, and recognition for student achievements in various competitions and events. Our vibrant extracurricular program offers a wide array of clubs and activities, ranging from academic clubs and cultural organizations to sports teams and community service initiatives.

These opportunities allow students to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and build connections with peers and mentors outside of the classroom. At Thomas Edison CTE High School, we are committed to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in college, careers, and beyond.
