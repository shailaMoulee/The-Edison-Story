What programs of study does Thomas Edison CTE High School offer?

We offer a variety of career and technical education programs, including computer science, engineering, health sciences, culinary arts, and more. Students can choose pathways that align with their interests and career goals.

Are there opportunities for students to earn industry certifications?

Yes, many of our programs offer opportunities for students to earn industry-recognized certifications, providing them with valuable credentials and enhancing their employability upon graduation.

What support services are available for students with diverse needs?

We provide comprehensive support services, including academic counseling, special education accommodations, English language learner support, and access to mental health resources to ensure that all students receive the assistance they need to succeed.

What extracurricular activities are available to students?

Our school offers a wide range of clubs and organizations, including academic clubs, cultural clubs, sports teams, and community service initiatives. Students can explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and build friendships outside of the classroom.

How does Thomas Edison CTE High School prepare students for college and career readiness?

We offer rigorous academic coursework, hands-on learning experiences, internships, and partnerships with industry professionals to prepare students for post-secondary education and careers in high-demand fields. Our goal is to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and experiences needed to thrive in college, careers, and beyond.
