About Us

At Thomas Edison Career and Technical Education High School, we are committed to fostering an environment of excellence, innovation, and inclusivity. With a rich history of academic achievement and a diverse student body, we strive to empower every individual to reach their full potential and make a positive impact on their community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire lifelong learners who are equipped with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to thrive in an ever-changing world. By providing a dynamic and supportive learning environment, we aim to cultivate leaders who are prepared to tackle the challenges of tomorrow and make meaningful contributions to society.
